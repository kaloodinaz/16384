About the 16384 Game

16384 is a brain-bending puzzle game inspired by the classic 2048. The objective is to combine matching number tiles to reach the ultimate goal—16384. Swipe or use arrow keys to move tiles on a 4x4 grid. When two tiles with the same value collide, they merge and double in number.

What starts as a simple swipe puzzle quickly becomes a strategic challenge. You’ll need to think ahead, plan your moves, and avoid filling the board with unmovable tiles. With each step closer to 16384, the pressure increases.

Whether you’re a seasoned puzzle master or a new player, 16384 offers a satisfying gameplay experience that’s easy to learn but tough to master.

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys (or swipe on mobile) to move tiles.
  • Combine tiles with the same number to double their value.
  • Strategize to keep space open and avoid dead ends.
  • Reach the 16384 tile to win!

Game Features

  • Clean, responsive design for all devices.
  • Engaging and challenging gameplay loop.
  • Inspired by the popular 2048 mechanics.
  • Unlimited plays – no downloads or signups.
